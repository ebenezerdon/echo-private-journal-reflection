# Echo - Private Journal & Reflection

A beautiful, local-first journaling application that offers AI-powered reflections without compromising privacy. Built by [Teda.dev](https://teda.dev), the AI app builder for everyday problems.

## Features

- **Local-First Security**: All entries are stored in your browser's LocalStorage. Nothing is sent to a server.
- **AI Reflections**: Uses WebLLM (running locally in your browser via WebGPU) to offer gentle insights on your entries.
- **Distraction-Free Writing**: A clean, minimalist interface focused on your thoughts.
- **Search**: Instantly filter through your past entries.

## Technology Stack

- **HTML5 & Tailwind CSS**: For structure and styling.
- **jQuery**: For DOM manipulation and state management.
- **WebLLM**: For client-side AI inference.
- **LocalStorage**: For data persistence.

## How to Use

1. Open `index.html` to view the landing page.
2. Click "Start Writing" or "Open App" to enter the application.
3. Create a new entry (+ button).
4. Write your thoughts.
5. Click "Reflect with AI" to get an on-device AI generated insight about your entry (Requires WebGPU capable browser).
